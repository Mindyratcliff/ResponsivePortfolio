Web Development

My interest in computers and technology dates back to being a kid, watching my dad use MS-Dos and Windows 3.1. I wanted to learn more about how they worked, and I spent a lot of my time playing games. As I got older, I began to play on websites and tried to make my own -- remember Geocities?! I learned HTML in bits and pieces through the years, and in 2017, I began dabbling in CSS, JavaScript, and a little Java. I finally signed up for a Boot Camp through The Ohio State University College of Engineering, and I am learning so much. It's a lot of fun, and I look forward to sharing the skills I gain with you!
